"We are what we Eat"

From the birth of mankind, our connection with food has been inseparable. In times of scarcity, we transform into ravenous beasts, devouring any organic matter we can find. Yet, when food is abundant, we employ diverse cooking techniques, combining and innovating to create a variety of delectable dishes that delight our souls. This relationship with food has not only sustained us but has also fostered the vibrant and diverse human civilization we know today.

The food comes in a variety of flavors:

  • Bitter
  • Salty
  • Sour
  • Spicy
  • Sweet

Human use various way to cook foods:

  1. Baking
  2. Boiling
  3. Deep-frying
  4. Grilling
  5. Microwaving
  6. Roasting
  7. Simmering
  8. Steaming
  9. Stewing
  10. Stir-frying
